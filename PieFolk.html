Pie Folk

Name: The Pie Folk
Nicknames: Cuties, Tasty Little People, Adorable Ones
Types: Food, Bipedal, Plantigrade, Humanoid
Level: 1
Diet: Herbivore
Preferred Food: Berries
Handler: No one
Languages: English

Special Containment Procedures: None.

Notes:

  1. They are free to roam around. There are tunnels in the walls that allow them to travel throughout the compound.
  2. They usually live on a Pietle, but the Pietles need to be brought in when someone is getting some outside time.

Personality: Representing a miniature society, the Pie Folk exhibit a range of personalities, although a shared aspect of their nature is their veneration of humans as deities who can provide them with a genuine demise.

Appearance: The Pie Folk's physical appearance closely resembles that of regular humans, except for their pie-like heads.

Warnings:

  1. They are very weak, so be EXTREMELY CAREFUL when handling them.

Abilities:

  1. None.

Room: An Outsider level 3 room.

Room Number: 36

Permissions: The Pie Folk are granted unrestricted access to various areas of the facility. However, if they request to be consumed, consumption should not occur unless authorized by a higher-ranking official.

History/Info: The initial discovery of Pie Folk took place on a remote Galapagos island. The initial interaction between researchers and the Pie Folk was marked by initial hostility, with the diminutive inhabitants wielding miniature spears and launching them as javelins. After a period of negotiation, the Pie Folk agreed to accompany the researchers. Following their settlement in the containment cell, the Pie Folk shared elements of their religious beliefs, expressing their view of humans as deities. Their initial panic subsided, leading to profuse apologies and ongoing deferential behavior, including bowing to any human who acknowledges them.
