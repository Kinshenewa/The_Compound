Pastel Bunny

Name: Pastel Bunny
Nicknames: Swear Storm, Asshole, Crazy Bunny, Jacksepticeye's Bunny
Types: Leporidae, Bipedal, Digitigrade, Vertebrae
Level: 3
Diet: Folivore
Preferred Food: Spicy Peppers
Handler: N/A
Languages: English

Special Containment Procedures: Sedation.

Notes:

  1. He shares a body with the Yellow Chick, the 'Adorable Miracle'.

Personality: He acts like Jacksepticeye, hence the nickname 'Jacksepticeye's Bunny'. He speaks with a consistently high volume and a strong mix of British and Irish accents, frequently utilizing explicit language. Although he rarely displays it, he has a caring side, evident when he speaks sweetly to his counterpart when he believes no one is listening.

Appearance: He is marbled pink, blue, and yellow. The front is more pink while the back is more blue. He has a fluffy white stomach and black eyes and two whiskers on each side. He has a cute little pink nose and black extremities.

Warnings:

  1. Profuse swearing is common in his language.
  2. He can get aggressive sometimes.

Abilities:

  1. He can switch with his counterpart, the Yellow Chick. First feet come out of the mouth and they turn completely inside out. They are vulnerable for the first few seconds, then solidify and become a regular creature.

Room: Regular level 3 room.

Room Number: 365

Permissions: He is allowed out thrice a week, and anything he finds will be inspected for safety. Once a week he is allowed to wander around the compound with a Helper, who will keep him out of areas he is not allowed into.

History/Info: He was discovered a year after the Yellow Chick's containment. She was relaxed in her room, then sighed before her beak started opening wider and wider. She started to implode on herself as a bright lavender and pink blob of flesh came out of her mouth, and before long she was gone. A moment later, the blob formed into the round shape of the Pastel Bunny, who proceeded to swear a storm about how bland the room was and that there should be more to keep his sister occupied. He demanded toys for his sister and that, in his words, "Someone bring in a fucking hamster wheel or something, this fucking room sucks balls and I won't fuckking stand for my sister to be bored out of her fucking mind!" The Pastel Bunny is quite strong-willed compared to his sister, who would do anything she's told to most of the time.
