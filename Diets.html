Diets

Here at the Compound, we have a variety of entities housed here, all with their own needs and diets. We ensure our entities are well-fed and happy, and to do that, they need to be fed the right things.

  • Carnivores - animal based diet
  • Herbivores - plant based diet
  • Omnivores - plant and animal based diet
  • Avivores - bird based diet
  • Insectivores - insect based diet
  • Bacterivores - bacteria based diet
  • Frugivores - fruit based diet
  • Folivores - leaf based diet
  • Piscivores - fish based diet
  • Photovores - Water and sunlight based diet
  • Photocarnivore - Meat and sunlight based diet, with no need for water
  • Allivore - capable of eating anything
  • Datavore - When the Entity consumes data
  • Zapvore - When an Entity consumes electricity
