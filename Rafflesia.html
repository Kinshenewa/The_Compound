Rafflesia

Name: Rafflesia
Nicknames: Strange Creature, Mind Reader, Forteller
Types: Draco, Quadrupedal, Digitigrade, Mythical, Vertebrae
Level: 7
Diet: Photovore
Preferred Food: Cantaloupes
Handler: N/A
Languages: English

Special Containment Procedures: Rafflesia displays extreme sensitivity and anxiety, requiring a calming presence in times of distress. Her episodes of apprehension primarily occur when she anticipates unfavorable events. In such instances, reassurance and comfort are effective strategies to alleviate her fears.

Notes:

  1. She has a fragile mental state, so be cautious when around her. She appears to have experienced a difficult life and is very claustrophobic.
  2. A peculiar bond seems to have formed between Rafflesia and the Shadow King, often prompting her attempts to interact with him.
  3. Cadence seems to calm her down considerably, so Cadence is required to visit Rafflesia at least twice a week for 4 hours. Cadence is now officially hired as a comfort Tiny for the monsters and not a technician.
  4. Rafflesia is visually impaired but compensates through the use of echolocation and her inherent powers.

Personality: Rafflesia is very fidgety and paranoid, always looking around. She rarely calms down, but her outbursts of concern for someone are not uncommon.

Appearance: Rafflesia possesses a lithe and highly flexible physique adorned with small, iridescent spots beneath her wings. Her scales reflect her emotions, typically displaying hues of dark purples, greens, and blues.

Warnings:

  1. Exercise discretion in your thoughts when near Rafflesia, as she is extraordinarily perceptive.

Abilities:

  1. Rafflesia possesses the unique ability to read the thoughts of those around her.
  2. She can foresee future events, although the specifics of this power remain obscure.
  3. Chromatic Scale Manipulation: Her scales can shift in coloration based on her emotional state.
  4. She can spray out a black substance that is like acid but dries hard.
  5. Echolocation: Despite her visual impairment, Rafflesia effectively employs echolocation to navigate and orient herself.

Room: A regular level 7 room.

Room Number: 2022

Permissions: Once a week she can go outside and practice her venom aim. Afterwards, we take some samples to analyze its composition, which appears to be a mixture of H₂SO₄ (Sulfuric acid) and HSI (Hydrochloric acid), a highly corrosive substance eroding organic materials.

History/Info: Rafflesia was found deep in the Amazon Rainforest and appeared eager to come with our researchers, seemingly searching for a safe place. Her capture involved a temporary containment cell and a volunteer who shared her enthusiasm. Rafflesia is of uncertain origin, claiming a Rainwing and Nightwing lineage from a fictional series. She possesses unique powers and unexplained silver markings on her neck.
