Food

In our unwavering commitment to the well-being of both our dedicated Handlers and unique Entities, we take a profoundly mindful approach to nutrition. Our culinary philosophy revolves around providing sustenance that is not only wholesome and natural but also safe for all involved.

Our cuisine boasts an all-natural foundation, free from the additives and chemicals commonly found in processed food. This commitment to pure, unadulterated ingredients ensures that every meal is prepared with the utmost care and attention to quality.

Every meal, by default, includes a serving of milk, not presented in a glass as this could pose safety concerns. This precaution prevents potential harm to both Handlers and Entities, as glassware can inadvertently become hazardous. The reason behind milk's inclusion is as practical as it is nurturing. It provides a source of essential calcium, vital for maintaining strong bones and overall physical resilience. This becomes particularly important for Handlers, ensuring they are prepared for the physical demands of caring for their unique Entities.

We understand that each of our Handlers, along with their unique Entity companions, is distinct. Therefore, while there are default meal options, we also cater to the individual dietary needs and preferences of our Handlers and their respective Entities. Customized variations are crafted to accommodate these unique palates, ensuring optimal nutrition and satisfaction.

Default Handler Meal Options:

    Breakfast:
  • 4 waffles
  • 2 pieces of toast
  • Bacon
  • A serving of milk
    Lunch:
  • 2 meat and cheese sandwiches
  • Bowl of chips
  • A serving of milk
    Dinner:
  • 2 steaks
  • Bowl of mashed potatoes
  • Bowl of macaroni and cheese
  • A serving of milk

In embracing the essence of natural, healthful nourishment, we exemplify our dedication to the overall well-being of both Handlers and Entities. Through our culinary care and thoughtfulness, we strive to maintain a nurturing and fulfilling environment for all who reside within our unique facility.
