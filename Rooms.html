Rooms

At The Compound, we offer a variety of specialized rooms tailored to the unique needs and security requirements of our inhabitants. Here's an overview of our room types:

Minimum Security: These rooms have only one Helper assigned to keep a watch on the inhabitant.

Low Security: Rooms in this category have 2-4 Helpers assigned for added security and support.

Medium Security: With 5-7 Helpers assigned, medium-security rooms provide an elevated level of assistance and protection.

High Security: For inhabitants who require increased protection, high-security rooms are staffed with 8-10 Helpers.

Maximum Security: These rooms have 11 or more Helpers assigned to ensure the highest level of security and care.

Elemental Resistance: Rooms equipped with resistance measures designed to accommodate Entities with unique powers, offering added security.

Dawn: Rooms perpetually maintain a dawn-like atmosphere, providing a unique living environment.

Dusk: Inhabitants of dusk rooms experience a perpetual twilight setting, creating a distinct ambiance.

Outsider: Meant for inhabitants who spend a significant amount of time outside, these rooms are adapted to their lifestyle.

Exotic: Exotic rooms are reminiscent of a lush jungle, featuring beautiful flora, ample sunlight, and often containing a pond or water feature.

Bio-luminated: Room is mainly illuminated by bioluminescent items, such as plants and insects.
