Outside Time

The sprawling landscape designated for Outside Time, an area encompassing a generous square acre, serves as a cherished retreat where our Entities can immerse themselves in the wonders of the natural world. This designated haven features an assortment of enchanting elements that facilitate a harmonious blend of recreation and respite, allowing our remarkable inhabitants to revel in the splendor of the outdoors.

The environment, thoughtfully designed to cater to the specific needs and preferences of each Entity, boasts an impressive array of attributes. Towering trees provide ample shade and shelter, their branches reaching high into the sky, offering a sanctuary for our Entities to explore. A tranquil lake, adorned with glistening waters, invites those with an affinity for aquatic experiences to indulge in moments of serene reflection.

The presence of gentle hillocks adds to the scenic diversity, affording an ideal vantage point for those Entities seeking solitude or the opportunity to survey their surroundings. These natural elevations enhance the picturesque charm of the Outside Time area, providing breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

The frequency and duration of Outside Time varies in accordance with an Entity's assigned level. Generally, entities with lower levels are afforded more opportunities to savor the outdoor environment, allowing them to partake in the delights of the natural world more frequently. However, the behavior and demeanor of an Entity play an integral role in determining the extent of their outdoor privileges. Well-behaved Entities are granted more extensive access, while those exhibiting less favorable conduct may find their outdoor excursions curtailed.

To infuse novelty and maintain the vitality of the environment, a weekly routine is in place wherein Outside Time is temporarily halted. Fences enclosing the area are carefully lowered, allowing local wildlife to roam freely and interact with our beloved inhabitants. This interplay with local fauna results in the introduction of new elements and discoveries for our Entities to experience. Furthermore, they may encounter a variety of objects and materials, the appropriation of which is subject to their conduct. Prudent and respectful behavior enhances the likelihood of bringing newfound treasures into our compound, thus contributing to the ever-evolving dynamics of the facility.

In essence, Outside Time transcends mere recreation. It is an opportunity for our Entities to bask in the beauty of nature, fostering a profound sense of connection and serenity. It is a manifestation of our commitment to maintaining a harmonious coexistence between our unique inhabitants and the natural world that surrounds them.
