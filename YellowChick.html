Yellow Chick

Name: Yellow Chick
Nicknames: Adorable Miracle, Cuteness, Cutey Wutey Chicky Wicky
Types: Aves, Bipedal, Digitigrade, Vertebrae
Level: 1
Diet: Omvivore
Preferred Food: Sweets
Handler: N/A
Languages: English

Special Containment Procedures: None, she's just a cute smoll bean.

Notes:

  1. She shares the same body with the Pastel Bunny, the 'Swear Storm'.
  2. Yellow Chick was discovered indulging in chocolates left unattended in an employee break room. She developed a preference for sweets and often requests more, displaying impeccable behavior. While she initially expressed remorse for consuming the candy, she continues to request more.
  3. Notably, Yellow Chick has exhibited growth during her stay at the Compound, evolving from a round bird-like form, humorously referred to as a "borb," to her current height of 3.5 feet. It is theorized that she is a juvenile entity and may continue to grow.

Personality: She is very kind and gentle and is very cooperative, unlike her counterpart.

Appearance: Standing at a height of 3.5 feet, she features bright yellow plumage with a slightly darker underbelly. Her wings and hair are exceptionally fluffy, and she possesses a petite orange beak. Her eyes are black, accentuated with delicate eyelashes, and her extremities are marked by black coloring.

Warnings:

  1. Beware her counterpart.

Abilities:

  1. She can switch with her counterpart, the Pastel Bunny. First feet come out of the mouth and they turn completely inside out. They are vulnerable for the first few seconds, then solidify and become the other.

Room: She has a regular level 3 room.

Room Number: 365

Permissions: She has been allowed to go into the yard in the afternoons and can bring in anything she wants. She can wander freely about the compound with a Helper staying near to ensure she doesn't wander into somewhere dangerous.

History/Info: Yellow Chick shares her existence with the Pastel Bunny and was originally discovered in a cornfield. It is believed that her origin is tied to a meteor impact in the vicinity a week before her discovery. Throughout her time at the Compound, Yellow Chick has displayed remarkable cooperation and well-behaved tendencies. Despite rarely making requests, her fondness for candy emerged when she was found gorging on chocolates in the break room. She has consistently displayed contentment with her presence at the Compound, showing no inclination to leave and a preference for remaining on-site.
