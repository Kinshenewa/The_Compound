Spectra

Name: Spectra
Nicknames: N/A
Types: Aves, Mythical, Digitigrade, Bipedal,
Level: 3
Diet: Photovore
Preferred Food: Fruits and fish
Handler: N/A
Languages: Swedish, English

Special Containment Procedures: Sedation if Helpers fail to persuade Entity to return to room.

Notes:

  1. Her feathers change color based on her mood.
  2. She has some light changing powers or something, she can make rainbows, reminds me of the symbol of Pink Floyd or whatever their name was.

Personality: She is very sociable and loves attention. She seems quite fond of when the researchers give her treats, and she has shown to be quite docile. Technically a Level 4, her amiable personality and good behavior has given her a Level of 3. She has been shown to adore playing with others, and has been given permission to interact with visitors during tours.

Appearance: She resembles a scarlet macaw, being 4 feet in height when standing tall. Her feathers hold the appearance of glass, clinking delicately when she moves, but change colors depending on lighting and her mood. Her beak is a deep maroon yet can direct rainbows to create interesting displays in the air or on a wall. Her feathers are shiny and reflective, being a dull grey when in pure darkness.

Warnings:

  1. She can get too excited sometimes, so it is recommeneded to never take off the specially made goggles designed to block out her bright flashes, lest she accidentally blind you.

Abilities:

  1. She is able to make small explosions with her feathers, the explosions never bigger than a tennis ball but able to cause serious damage, almost seeming to delete anything that is affected by it. Experimentation revealed that when flesh was deleted, the flesh closed as if never there in the first place.
  2. She can get quite excited in the morning, and her chirps and chattering can be deafening when combined with her extremely bright feathers. Never take off your ear muffs in range of her.
  3. She has no need to eat, instead being able to convert sunlight into energy and only needing water every few days. though she is supplied with a small creek that is safe for her to drink and bathe in.

Room: Exotic Dusk room.

Room Number: 500

Permissions: She is allowed to accompany visitors on tours after every visitor has been supplied the spaciel goggles and earmuffs.

History/Info: She had been found in Sweden attempting to nest in a lighthouse beacon, having shown great joy in standing in the light and charging up her feathers. She had picked up Swedish by listening to people passing by the lighthouse, and after a month housed in the Compound, she was able to pick up English as well. Intelligence tests later revealed she has an IQ of 114, an above average score, and that was shown in her actions when she was able to figure out how to escape her room using the pinpad, which required a pass-code that researchers had never let her see.
