Name: Glimmerstrike
Nicknames: Sparkleglows, little glow snake
Types: Serpentes, Bioluminescent
Level: 6
Diet: Carnivore
Preferred Food: Bioluminescent sea creatures
Handler: Aria Starfall

Special Containment Procedures:

Notes:

  1. Glimmerstrike is a solitary and nocturnal creature.
  2. Requires periodic access to a shallow pool of water for hydration.
  3. Possesses retractable bioluminescent claws.

Personality: Glimmerstrike is a patient and observant entity. It is known for its agility and precision when hunting, but it can be skittish in the presence of bright lights or loud noises.

Appearance: Glimmerstrike has iridescent scales that shift in color from deep blue to silvery white. It has slender, serpentine body with a long, prehensile tail. Its claws emit a soft, radiant glow, and its eyes are adapted for night vision.

Warnings:

  1. Do not expose Glimmerstrike to intense light, as it can cause stress.
  2. Avoid sudden loud noises, which can startle Glimmerstrike.

Abilities:

  1. Bioluminescent Claws: Glimmerstrike's claws emit a soft, radiant glow that it can control. It uses this bioluminescence to attract prey or communicate with other Glimmerstrikes.
  2. Exceptional Camouflage: Glimmerstrike can blend seamlessly into its surroundings, making it nearly invisible to the naked eye.
  3. Silent Movement: It moves without making a sound, allowing it to stalk prey silently.

Room: Elemental resistance, Bio-luminated Level 6 room

Room Number: 442

Permissions: N/A, Glimmerstrike is unable to leave its room without freaking out due to the lighting level.
